ABOUT CATALOG CONTACT

Ruckus Music Publishing is dedicated to producing practical, beautiful, editions of music that has been unavailable or difficult to obtain. We focus on new music by living composers, out-of-print works, and music that has not been published in accessible performance editions. Offering both fine bound printed scores and downloadable digital editions, we aim to provide increased access to previously unavailable music in editions that are well-researched, carefully edited, and designed for practical performance use.
